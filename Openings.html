Openings

for motivated students and researchers

Ph.D. Students

We are looking for potential PhD students for enrollment in Fall 2020. A solid background in math is preferred. The research project is on computational solid mechanics and material science. For more information regarding our research, please visit Dr. Cao's research page. Potential candidates are encouraged to send a copy of their resume and TRANSCRIPTS to Dr. Cao (leicao@unr.edu) for funding opportunities.

Master's Students

We are always interested in motivated thesis-oriented master's students. A solid background in math is preferred. The research project is on computational solid mechanics and material science. For more information regarding our research, please visit Dr. Cao's research page. Potential candidates are encouraged to send a copy of their resume and TRANSCRIPTS to Dr. Cao (leicao@unr.edu).

Undergraduate Students

We are also enthusiastic in working with talented undergraduate researchers. Feel free to contact Dr. Cao regarding the availability of research positions and the ongoing exciting research projects in our group. For more information regarding our research, please visit Dr. Cao's research page. Potential candidates are encouraged to send a copy of their resume to Dr. Cao (leicao@unr.edu).

Postdocs and Visiting Scholars

We also welcome postdocs (depending on funding availability) and self-funded visiting scholars to join our lab if there is mutual research interest between us. For more information regarding our research, please visit Dr. Cao's research page. Potential candidates are encouraged to send a copy of their detialed CV to Dr. Cao (leicao@unr.edu).
